## Design Decisions

### Decision 1: Keep strategy logic identical across MCP providers
**Rationale:** Same decision criteria, output format, and risk management regardless of MCP. Simplifies provider swapping.

**Tradeoff:** Forces Massive instruction workarounds for Greeks/SQL into manual code for AV.

### Decision 2: Maintain Phase 1/2/3 structure
**Rationale:** Consistent naming and flow makes provider swapping intuitive.

**Alternative:** Optimize each provider's tool discovery separately. **Rejected:** Would require maintaining different conceptual models.

### Decision 3: AV has built-in technicals; Massive requires `apply` functions
**Rationale:** Leverage provider strengths. AV's built-in BBANDS and RSI are more efficient than Massive's SMA/EMA + manual Bollinger.

### Decision 4: Manual Greeks estimation for AV
**Rationale:** No built-in Black-Scholes in AV. Provide estimation guidance in instructions rather than hard fail.
